VINSMOKE SANJI

Sanji is a cook and a fighter in the Straw Hat Pirates, a group
of adventurers in the manga and anime series One Piece123. He joined
the crew in the East Blue, where he worked at an ocean-going restaurant
called Baratie. He has a secret history as a member of the Vinsmoke family,
a powerful and ruthless clan of assassins45. He is known for his Black-leg Style,
a martial art that uses only his legs, and his love for women. He is one of the
strongest and most notorious members of the crew, along with Luffy and Zoro, who are
also called the "Monster Trio". He dreams of finding the All Blue , a legendary sea
that contains all kinds of fish.
